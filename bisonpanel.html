BISON PANEL CEMENT SHEETS

Ideal for commercial, industrial or residential usage, the Dry Walls are a boon to architects, offering the best in protection, safety, robustness and versatility. These are easy to install and require virtually no maintenance, thus offering the tremendous twin-benefits of time and cost savings.

BISON DESIGNER

Bison Designer boards are specially suitable for wall claddings and false ceilings. Designer Boards are available in size 8' x4' (2440mm x 1220mm) and 6mm, 8mm, 10mm, 12mm & 16mm thicknesses.

BISONLAM

Prelaminated Bison Panels, a cement bonded particle board, are manufactured under the Brand name BISON Lam.Bison Lam contributes to exterior grade qualities like fire, water, termite and fungus resistance of the plain Bison Panel with a beautiful look and finishes of a laminated board.Bison Lam is available in thicknesses viz., 6 mm, 10 mm, 12 mm and 16 mm in shades like Natural Teak, Wenge, Maple & Pine.

BISON PLANKS

Bison plank is specially made for exterior and interior wooden finish look. Available in 8’x6” size.
